NestX is New Zealand's first private home swap marketplace. You list your home, find another homeowner who wants what you have, and swap — paying only the difference in value directly to each other. No agent. No commission. Every dollar of profit stays with you.

How it works
1
List your homePost your property with photos, price and what you're looking for in a swap.
2
Find your matchBrowse other listings and send a swap offer — upsizing, downsizing, or a lateral swap.
3
Agree the differenceIf your homes are different values, the lower-value owner pays the difference. A conveyancer handles both transfers on the same day.
