Pricing questions

Why is the fee based on RV and not asking price?

RV is independently set by your local council and is publicly verifiable — which means it's harder to misrepresent. We verify all RVs before listings go live.

What happens if my RV is much lower than my asking price?

That's completely normal in NZ — many properties sell well above RV. Your fee is based on RV only, not your asking price or final sale price.

Can I get a refund if I change my mind?

Registration fees are non-refundable. If you remove your listing, your access ends immediately. There are no monthly fees so there is nothing further to cancel.

Is there any commission when my swap or sale completes?

No — NestX charges zero commission. Like TradeMe, we are a private listing platform. You deal directly with the other party. If you choose to engage a real estate agent that is entirely your decision — but with NestX you never have to.

What does the Swap or Sell upgrade include?

For $199 your listing is upgraded to accept both swap offers and straight purchase offers. This maximises your chances of a result and is available anytime after registration.
