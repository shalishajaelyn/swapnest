Why you need a conveyancer

Every property swap on Nest X must be completed through a licensed NZ conveyancer. They prepare the sale and purchase agreements, conduct title searches, coordinate with banks, and ensure both property transfers settle simultaneously and legally.

For a swap sale, we recommend choosing a conveyancer who has experience with simultaneous settlements — where two properties transfer ownership on the same day. Not all conveyancers handle these regularly, so it's worth asking upfront.

💡 Tip: Both parties in a swap can use different conveyancers — in fact, it's recommended so each party has independent legal representation.
