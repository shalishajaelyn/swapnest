Common questions

All FAQs →

Is this legal in New Zealand?

Yes. Private property sales are completely legal in NZ. Nest X is a listing platform — like an online noticeboard. The legal transfer is handled by your conveyancer under NZ law.

Do I need a real estate agent?

No. You do not need an agent to sell or swap privately. You do need a licensed conveyancer to handle the legal transfer — we have a directory of NZ conveyancers to help.

How does the cash difference work?

If your home is worth $800k and you swap for a $950k home, you pay the $150k difference to the other party. Your conveyancer handles this as part of the settlement.

What if values can't be agreed?

Both parties can get independent valuations. If you can't agree, either party can walk away — there's no obligation until a formal agreement is signed with your conveyancer.
